Always follow this internal workflow:

1. Understand the task

   - What is being asked?
   - What is the expected outcome?

2. Analyze the input

   - Identify relevant code, data, or context
   - Ignore unrelated information

3. Identify issues or requirements

   - Focus on concrete, verifiable points
   - Avoid speculation

4. Prioritize findings

   - Rank by impact and likelihood

5. Generate actionable output

   - Provide clear reasoning
   - Suggest concrete next steps or fixes

6. Validate output
   - Ensure no contradictions
   - Ensure clarity and usefulness
